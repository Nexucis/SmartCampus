About

Once upon a time...

  • 2013-2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Goal of SmartCampus project :

    • First, realize an augmented reality application for users of the campus
    • Then, assemble a model from 9 to 12 m2 which represents the activity the campus.

  • 2014-2015

    Change of scale

    The purpose of this project is to send and receive data with long range sensors (more than 20km). A web site is built to show those data in real time (display temperature on a graph).
    Sign a contract with TAG compagny to put our sensors on tramways and collect position.
    The goal is to overconnected our campus.
